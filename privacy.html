Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Hey Toru! is built on one principle: your thoughts belong to you. This privacy policy explains exactly what we do and do not do with your data — in plain language, not legal fog.

What We Collect

Hey Toru! does not collect personal information. We do not require an account, email address, or any form of registration to use the app.

Your Notes

Every note you capture is stored only on your device using Apple's secure local storage. Your notes never leave your device automatically. They are never uploaded to any server, never backed up to our systems, and never shared with anyone.

If you choose to use the Backup feature, your notes are saved as a file that you control entirely. You decide where it goes — Files, iCloud, email, or anywhere else. We never receive or store this file.

Your Voice

When you use the Speak feature, your voice is processed entirely on your device by Apple's iOS speech recognition system. Hey Toru! never records, stores, or transmits your voice or audio to any server.

Text Processing

When you tap Catch it, the text you have typed or spoken is sent securely to Hey Toru!'s server for intelligent structuring. This is the only data that leaves your device. Once your structured note is returned, this text is immediately discarded from our server. It is never stored, never logged, and never used for any other purpose.

Your Name

If you enter your name during onboarding, it is stored only on your device. It is never sent to any server.

What We Do Not Do

Hey Toru! does not display advertisements. Hey Toru! does not sell your data. Hey Toru! does not use tracking or analytics. Hey Toru! does not share any information with third parties. Hey Toru! does not use cookies or any form of cross-app tracking.

Children

Hey Toru! is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy in a meaningful way, we will notify you through the app. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.

Contact

Questions about privacy? We are happy to talk. Reach us at info@heytoru.com.
