Common Questions

How do I capture a note?

Tap Speak and talk naturally — messy is welcome. Or type directly into the Catch Box. Then tap Catch it. Hey Toru! will structure your thought into a clean note.

My voice is not being understood correctly

For best results, use AirPods or hold the phone close. Make sure your Notes In language matches the language you are speaking. Move somewhere quieter if there is background noise.

How do I change the note language?

Go to the You tab, scroll to Notes In, and select your language. Hey Toru! will write all your notes in that language from that point on — regardless of what language you speak.

Where are my notes stored?

Everything is stored only on your device. Nothing is sent to any cloud automatically. Your notes are private by design.

How do I back up my notes?

Go to the You tab, scroll to Backup and Restore, and tap Backup Now. You can save your backup to Files, iCloud, email, or anywhere you choose.

What is Toru Smart?

Toru Smart is our top tier — it includes Red/Green task lists, 7-day intelligent intervention, smart reminders, shared access, pattern recognition, and The Bridge. Available for $18/month.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to iPhone Settings, tap your Apple ID, tap Subscriptions, and manage Hey Toru! from there. Your notes are always yours regardless of plan.
